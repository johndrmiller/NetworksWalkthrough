Student messages can be accessed through the Messages icon. When accessed, this icon displays student messages. Access to an individual student message is available by clicking the message or clicking See All.
To search content by topic or standard, click the Search Icon. 
Click Chapter/Lesson to open a dropdown menu. 
Each lesson plan is aligned to the standards and is customizable. Lesson plans are also divided into sections that follow the teaching cycle of “Engage→Teach and Assess→Close and Reflect.” Click the arrow adjacent to a section of the teaching cycle to open it. 
Interactive activities, printable worksheets, and links to lesson resources display. A resource can be launched by clicking it.
When clicked, Customize allows notes to be edited, revised, rearranged, and saved as a custom lesson plan that can be printed. Teachers can also print copies of the lecture notes.
Activities within each lesson are identified if they are appropriate for Approaching Level (AL) or Beyond Level (BL) students, and whether they are appropriate for English Language Learners (ELL).

Before you start your digital review, there are a few things to take care of:
