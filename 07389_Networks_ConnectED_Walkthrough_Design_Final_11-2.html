Recent assignments can be viewed by clicking Manage & Assign.

Click Messages to view student messages. After the student messages display, click the message or click See All to view the full message.
Each lesson plan is aligned to the standards and is customizable. Lesson plans are also divided into sections that follow the teaching cycle of “Engage→Teach and Assess→Close and Reflect.” Click the arrow adjacent to a section of the teaching cycle to open it. 
Interactive activities, printable worksheets, and links to lesson resources display. A resource can be launched by clicking it.
When clicked, Customize allows notes to be edited, revised, rearranged, and saved as a custom lesson plan that can be printed. Teachers can also print copies of the lecture notes.

Teacher Center Plan and Present
Lesson plans also contain activities and questions that use the student edition content in the lesson. Activities are coded by the type of skills they help to develop.

Teacher Center Assign

The Online Assessment Center displays all the chapter tests and lesson quizzes. Use the existing test questions, edit them to meet classroom needs, or create questions and tests from scratch.

Resources can be uploaded to the Resource Library at any time by clicking My Files.
